Orange Chicken

The variety of orange chicken most commonly found at North American Chinese restraunts consists of chopped,battered and fried chicken pieces coated in a sweet orange-flavored chilli sauce, which then thickens or caramelizes to a glaze and is served with other dishes

Ingredients

Chicken:

  • 4 Boneless Skinless Chicken Breasts cut into bite-size pieces
  • 3 Eggs whisked
  • ⅓ cup Cornstarch
  • ⅓ cup Flour
  • Oil for frying
